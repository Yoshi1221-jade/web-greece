Greece is a stunning country in southeastern Europe, known as the cradle of Western civilization. Famous for its ancient history, breathtaking islands, and vibrant culture, Greece boasts iconic landmarks like the Acropolis in Athens and the whitewashed villages of Santorini.

Official Name: Hellenic Republic (Ελληνική Δημοκρατία / Ellinikí Dimokratía)

Location: Southeastern Europe, at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa.

Capital: Athens (3+ million people in metro area).

Population: ~10.4 million (2024 estimate).

Language: Greek (official), but English widely spoken in tourist areas.

Government: Unitary parliamentary republic.

Currency: Euro (€) (since 2001).
